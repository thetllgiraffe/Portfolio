About Me

My journey to code began in high school. My days outside of the classroom were spent messing around and creating my own modifications for the games I loved to play such as Skyrim, Minecraft, and the Fallout series. As I entered college I gained new skills in the development of website software. This new opportunity was the start of my professional career and down the rabbit hole I went. I discovered a passion for taking on an assortment of ideas and requests from small businesses and friends, personal projects, to corporations needing a fresh sense of creativity.

During this period, COVID-19 took hold, and whilst this presented many hurdles in daily life relatable to others who've struggled, it was the perfect moment to dig in and embrace my career as a developer bringing me to today. Nowadays you can find websites with charming, efficient, and unique interfaces for users to see and enjoy.

However, I don't live a sedentary life, and so you can also find me messing around with hardware and learning more on how machines work 💻, gaming 🎮, or outside looking for new trails to hike 🥾.
