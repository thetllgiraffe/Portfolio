Site Shop

Currently I am in the process of developing 2 different websites: the Event Hoster and Templately. Both of these websites are spearheaded by myself and involve working with a range of experienced developers sourced from the 100Devs community. Each site implements private APIs, responsive and fluid website dynamics, and bold themes. But what does each site do?

Event Hoster, as the name implies, is a website that fetches information from top hosting companies and social media posts to bring a list of both ongoing and future plans. We are currently experimenting with an AI model to help sift through repeat posts mentioning the same event under different names. Users can also post and create thier own events and we are working on incorporating our data with platforms such as Calendy and local government search sites to automatically display these events.

Templately is a trailblazer project. In contrast to all other template websites for developing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript templates, this site is intended to not only conglomerate thier data and provide links to these exterior websites, but create filters such as source, costs, and more. Essentially the Trivago of templates. Furthermore, a planned revenue generator using a basic free plan and a VIP membership is in the works for manually creating your own template or searching based on characteristics such as color themes, where elements lay on the page, and fill-in-the-blank interactivity.
