Services

What I Provide

In my professional career and at 100Devs I create both fluent, responsive, elastic fullstack web applications and teach others skills, semantics, and how to write clean code. I build from scratch and off existing templates to bring your websites to life. I also forge new opportunities to help break others into tech with roles in website development from the start of an idea to the maintenance of a corporate server. This list is not exhaustive but highlights the skills and provisions that many developers gloss over or don't incorporate.

Efficient Code

Creating a website is one thing but ensuring it runs smooth, fast, and keeps customers happy is key to success. I ensure all that I create and vouch for runs and transitions smoothly whether that be from a new website or contributions to an existing one.

Clean Writing

No eye-strain here. We don't write spaghetti code, this is lasagna with easy to read blocks, tags at proper line depths, and comments for future coders to easily identify and find where they need to go.

Rapid Onboarding

Finding a developer can prove difficult depending on the level of complexity of the project or which phase of development you are in. Whether you are just starting with an idea or are an established business, any part of the ladder can be climbed and worked from by myself and those I work with.

Support and Debugging

All developrs attempt to make your vision true, but technology changes and foundations erode without proper maintenance. Getting support to keep your website running and to help transition it over can feel intimidating but with the proper skills and coordination, can be done in short order.

Volunteer Opportunities

I work with a variety of individuals who come from all different aspects of life such as charity foundations, coorporations, and family businesses. I reach out to these entities to help others learn how to make a website and provide ample connections for the future.
